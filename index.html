About Casian IRF

Casian IRF (Industrial Resin Flooring) is Ireland’s trusted specialist for high-performance epoxy and polyurethane flooring systems. We combine expert surface preparation with premium materials to deliver seamless, chemical-resistant floors built to last.

Our certified installers handle projects of any scale—from manufacturing plants and warehouses to food processing facilities—ensuring on-time delivery, industry compliance, and a flawless finish every time.

Our Proven Process

1

Consultation & Quotation

We assess your facility and recommend the optimal resin system, tailored to your traffic, chemical exposure, and budget.

2

Surface Preparation

Remove contaminants, repair damage, and profile the concrete to ensure proper mechanical bonding of the coating.

3

Professional Application

Install primer, broadcast aggregates (if needed), and top-coat layers using industrial-grade rollers and trowels for uniform coverage.

4

Quality Assurance & Handover

After curing, we perform adhesion tests and a final inspection to ensure your floor meets our rigorous performance standards.
