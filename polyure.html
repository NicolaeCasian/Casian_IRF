Polyurethane Flooring

Polyurethane flooring combines flexibility with outstanding durability and UV stability. At Casian IRF, our high‐performance polyurethane coatings resist abrasion, thermal shock and chemicals—making them perfect for industrial plants, food processing, automotive shops and more.
