MMA Flooring

High-performance epoxy resin floors for industrial and commercial facilities, offering excellent durability and chemical resistance. MMA (Methyl Methacrylate) floors cure rapidly, making them ideal for fast turnaround environments and operational facilities that cannot afford long downtimes.

Benefits of MMA Flooring

  • Ultra-fast curing time — ready within hours
  • Exceptional durability under harsh conditions
  • Highly resistant to chemicals and solvents
  • Suitable for cold and sub-zero applications
  • Ideal for hygienic and sterile environments

Applications

MMA flooring is widely used in commercial kitchens, hospitals, pharmaceutical plants, cold storage areas, food processing, manufacturing facilities, and garages — anywhere fast installation and durability are critical.
