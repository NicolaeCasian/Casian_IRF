About Us

Casian Industrial Resin Flooring (Casian IRF) is a leading provider of high-quality industrial resin flooring solutions. We specialize in durable epoxy and polyurethane floor coatings for a variety of industrial and commercial environments.

Our experienced team ensures every project is executed with precision and professionalism, from surface preparation to final finish. We pride ourselves on delivering floors that are safe, easy to maintain, and built to withstand heavy use, all while meeting our clients’ aesthetic and performance requirements.

Our Process

1

Consultation

We discuss your needs and assess your facility to recommend the best resin flooring solution for your application.

2

Preparation

Our team preps the concrete substrate through cleaning, repairs, and profiling to ensure a perfect bond for the resin.

3

Application

We install the chosen resin flooring system with precision – whether epoxy, polyurethane, or a specialized coating – ensuring even coverage and thickness.

4

Finishing

After curing, we perform a thorough quality check, leaving you with a polished, safe, and ready-to-use floor that meets all your requirements.
