Floor Preparation

Proper floor preparation is essential for ensuring the longevity and performance of any resin flooring system. At Casian IRF, we use industry-leading techniques and equipment to remove old coatings, smooth uneven surfaces, and prepare concrete substrates for flawless application.

Benefits of Professional Floor Preparation

  • Maximizes adhesion of resin coatings
  • Eliminates dust, oil, and contaminants
  • Repairs cracks, joints, and surface damage
  • Improves leveling and surface smoothness
  • Ensures long-term durability and finish quality

Applications

Our preparation services are ideal for warehouses, production areas, commercial kitchens, garages, showrooms, and any location requiring a solid foundation for flooring systems.
