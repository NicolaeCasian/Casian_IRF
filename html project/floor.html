Floor Preparation

Proper floor preparation is the foundation of any high-performance resin system. Casian IRF’s expert team removes old coatings, contaminants, and weak surface layers—and profiles concrete to achieve optimal adhesion and a perfectly level substrate.

Precision grinding to smooth, level, and open concrete pores.

Scarifying

Aggressive mechanical scarifier for heavy removal and profiling.

Moisture Testing

Priming

High-penetration primers to seal and prepare porosity.
