Floor Preparation

Proper floor preparation is the foundation of any high-performance resin system. Casian IRF’s expert team removes old coatings, contaminants, and weak surface layers — and profiles concrete to achieve optimal adhesion and a perfectly level substrate.

Why Floor Preparation Matters

Removes contaminants & weak layers

Corrects surface irregularities

Optimizes final finish performance

Our Preparation Services

Shot Blasting

High-powered grit blasting to remove old coatings and profile concrete for superior mechanical bond.

Diamond Grinding

Precision grinding to smooth and level surfaces while opening pores for optimal adhesion.

Scarifying

Aggressive mechanical scarification for heavy-duty removal of coatings and contaminants.

Moisture Testing

Rapid in-situ relative humidity and moisture vapor emission rate assessments to prevent failures.

Self-Leveling

Flowable leveling compounds for a perfectly flat and even substrate before coating.

Priming

Penetrating primers to seal concrete, reduce porosity, and ensure uniform topcoat appearance.
